Executive Summary

MediaPro Adria will be established as a subsidiary of MediaPro International (Dubai) in Zagreb, Croatia, to capitalize on the growing media production market in the Adriatic region. The company will leverage equipment from Dubai that is underutilized during Croatia's peak tourist season (summer months), creating operational synergies and maximizing asset utilization across the global MediaPro network.

The subsidiary will be structured as a Limited Liability Company (d.o.o.) with an initial team of 10 employees, including key management positions of Regional Director, Sales Director, and Regional Head for Montenegro. MediaPro Adria will offer comprehensive media production services including video production, equipment rental, post-production, and live event coverage, with a special focus on the Montenegro market.

Business Entity Structure

Legal Form: Limited Liability Company (d.o.o.)

  • Minimum capital requirement of only €2,500
  • Limited liability protection for shareholders
  • Simplified management structure
  • Most common and recognized business form in Croatia
  • Suitable for small to medium-sized operations
  • Flexibility for future growth

MediaPro Adria will be 100% owned by MediaPro International (Dubai), operating as a wholly-owned subsidiary. This structure provides clear lines of control while maintaining operational flexibility.

Registration Requirements

  • Notarized Articles of Association
  • Minimum share capital of €2,500 deposited in a Croatian bank account
  • Registration with the Croatian Trade Register
  • Registration with tax authorities
  • Appointment of at least one director

The company registration process typically takes 2-3 weeks, with an additional 2-3 weeks for opening a bank account and completing other administrative requirements. The subsidiary can be operational within 6-8 weeks from initiating the registration process.

Equipment Utilization Plan

A key strategic advantage for MediaPro Adria will be the utilization of equipment from MediaPro International's Dubai inventory during Croatia's peak tourist season, which coincides with Dubai's slower summer period. This approach offers several benefits:

  • Reduced capital expenditure requirements for the new subsidiary
  • Higher utilization rate of existing equipment
  • Access to high-end production gear without the full investment cost
  • Flexibility to scale operations based on seasonal demand

Services Offered

  • Video Production Services: Full-service video production for commercials, corporate videos, documentaries, and entertainment content.
  • Equipment Rental: Professional-grade cameras, lighting, audio, and production equipment available for rent.
  • Post-Production Services: Editing, color grading, sound design, and visual effects.
  • Live Event Coverage: Technical production services for concerts, festivals, sports events, and corporate gatherings.
  • Montenegro Market Services: Specialized services tailored to the growing media market in Montenegro.

Revenue Forecast

MediaPro Adria's revenue is projected to grow from €1.12 million in Year 1 to €2.18 million by Year 5, representing a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of approximately 18%.

Revenue will be derived from five primary service categories, with Video Production Services representing the largest segment at 30.4% of revenue by Year 5.

Profitability Analysis

MediaPro Adria is projected to be profitable from Year 1, with steadily improving metrics:

  • Gross Profit: Increasing from €571,000 in Year 1 to €1,428,600 in Year 5
  • Net Profit: Growing from €468,220 in Year 1 to €1,171,452 in Year 5
  • Profit Margin: Improving from 41.8% in Year 1 to 53.5% by Year 5
  • ROI: Based on an initial investment of €250,000, ROI grows from 187.3% in Year 1 to 468.6% by Year 5
